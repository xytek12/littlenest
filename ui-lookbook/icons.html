LittleNest — Icon Concepts, Round 5

Six brand-forward directions. No moons, no watercolor, no literal babies.

Nestling — The Mascot
Original creature: a fat dumpling-owl hybrid with sleepy half-closed eyes, tiny ear tufts, and a warm honey glow. Huggable brand character energy — the LittleNest version of Huckleberry's "Berry."
Cradle Loop
Apple Fitness-level minimalism — a single thick cream ribbon sweeping into a cradling arm shape, with a soft dot floating inside the hold. Zero decoration, reads at 60px, bold on any home screen.
Holographic Infinity
Full-bleed iridescent oilslick gradient — magenta, cyan, gold, lavender faking a conic sweep. A white infinity mark floats at center with a glow halo. Apple Music Classical premium energy. Unique on any home screen.
Hanko Wax Seal
Vermillion stamp chop on warm washi paper — a carved spiral mark with ink pressure bleed. Feels handcrafted, ceremonial, precious. Like the official seal on your baby's existence.
Tarot — The Sun
Midnight navy tarot card with gold corner flourishes, radiating sun rays, "XIX" numeral, and a tiny arms-spread figure at center. Mystical, elegant, precious-child energy. Unlike anything else in the App Store.
Pixel Baby — 8-bit Charm
Retro 16px pixel-grid creature — sleepy half-lid eyes, blush cheeks, butter-yellow tuft, tiny arms raised. Stardew Valley / Game Boy palette. Nostalgic playfulness that stands out completely from every other baby app icon.
