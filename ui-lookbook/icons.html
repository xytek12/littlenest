LittleNest — Icon Concepts, Round 3

Six fresh directions. Designer notes below each.

Sleeping Fox
A storybook fox curled into the moon's crescent. Warm amber & deep navy — feels like an illustrated children's book cover at any size.
Watercolor Sketch
Hand-painted washes in pink, sage, and peach with ink-line moon and feeding spoon. Children's-book cover energy — warm and organic.
Liquid Glass
Apple visionOS / iOS 26 aesthetic — a translucent milky blob with rim-light shimmer, golden star and a silver spoon. Elegant dark canvas.
Sticker-Pop Crescent
Bold die-cut sticker aesthetic — coral crescent with hard drop shadow cradling a baby bottle, white border outlines, polka-dot background. High energy.
Constellation Baby
Star points connected by fine lines form a sleeping newborn on a deep-space canvas. Mysterious and elegant — reads beautifully small on a phone home screen.
Olive Branch Nest
Woven nest of olive branches holds a glowing egg with a star inside. Sage leaves + warm honey tones — "LittleNest" as literal visual wordplay, full of warmth.
